<Callout kind="info" title="Prerequisites">
Before starting, ensure you have:
- A valid email address for account registration
- A supported payment method (credit card, Mada, or Apple Pay)
- Access to Steam, Epic Games, or other platforms for game activation
</Callout>

## Create Your Vandar Account

Follow these steps to register quickly.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Vandar" icon="globe">
    Go to the [Vandar homepage](https://vandarstore.com).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Click **Sign Up** in the top right corner and fill in your details:
    - Email address
    - Password (at least 8 characters)
    - Phone number (Saudi number recommended)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email and click the link to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to the site and log in with your credentials.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Add to Cart and Checkout

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Product" icon="shopping-cart">
    Click **Add to Cart** on your chosen game.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Cart" icon="list">
    Go to your cart via the icon in the header. Adjust quantities if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Proceed to Checkout" icon="credit-card">
    Enter shipping details (digital delivery is instant) and select payment method.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Purchase" icon="check-circle">
    Pay securely and receive your code or account details immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
Payments are processed securely with Saudi gateways. Delivery is instant for digital products.
</Callout>

## Receive and Activate Your Purchase

After checkout, you'll get an email with activation instructions.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Steam Account" icon="package">
    1. Log in to Steam with provided credentials.
    2. Verify account ownership if prompted.
    3. Download and play the game.

    <Expandable title="Troubleshooting Steam Login">
      If login fails, ensure you're using the exact username and password. Contact support via the Vandar dashboard.
    </Expandable>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Activation Code" icon="key">
    1. Open the game launcher (e.g., EA App, Ubisoft Connect).
    2. Go to **Redeem Code**.
    3. Enter the code from your Vandar email.

  </Tab>
</Tabs>
